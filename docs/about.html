About Me

Melody, also known online as Aori (Aoriki), is an emerging digital artist developing a practice in interactive media and game creation.

Their work focuses on digital illustration, visual experimentation, and the early stages of game design, with an interest in atmosphere and narrative.

Over the coming year, they aim to strengthen their technical skills in game development and create more cohesive interactive projects that merge digital art with engaging gameplay.
